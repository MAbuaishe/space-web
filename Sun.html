What position is the Sun in our solar system?

The Sun is at the center of the solar system.


How old is the Sun?

4.6 billion years old


What is the Sun's atmosphere made of?

The Sun's atmosphere is composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, with trace amounts of other elements.


Potential for Life?

The Sun itself cannot support life, but its energy is crucial for life on Earth.


What is the gravity of the Sun compared to Earth?

The Sun's gravity is about 28 times stronger than Earth's gravity.


average distance between the Sun and Earth?

149.6 million kilometers (93 million miles).






surface of the Sun :
The surface of the Sun is called the photosphere, and it appears yellow from Earth.
Solar Flares:
Solar flares are explosive bursts of energy from the Sun's surface, impacting space weather.
Sunspots:
Sunspots are dark patches on the Sun's surface caused by magnetic activity.





Why is it difficult to explore the Sun?


Exploring the Sun is difficult due to its extreme temperatures, radiation, and violent activity.
No spacecraft can land on the Sun due to its gaseous state and intense heat.
