How old is Uranus?

What is the Uranus atmosphere made of?

Uranus' atmosphere is primarily composed of hydrogen, helium, and methane, giving it its blue color.


Potential for Life?

Uranus has little potential for life due to its extreme cold and lack of a solid surface.


What is the gravity on Uranus compared to Earth?

Uranus' gravity is about 89% of Earth's gravity, so you would weigh slightly less on Uranus.

surface of Uranus :
Uranus has a thick, icy atmosphere and is known for its extreme axial tilt of about 98 degrees, meaning it rotates on its side.
The Rings of Uranus:
Uranus has a faint ring system made up of dark particles.
Moons of Uranus:
Uranus has 27 known moons, including Miranda, Ariel, and Titania.





Why is it difficult to explore Uranus?


Uranus is difficult to explore due to its distance from Earth, extreme cold, and lack of a solid surface. No dedicated mission has yet been sent to Uranus, but NASA's Voyager 2 provided the most detailed images in 1986.
